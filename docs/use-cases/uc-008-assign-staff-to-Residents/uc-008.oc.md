## Operation Contract

### Open Staff Assignment Overview
- **Input**: Shift.
- **Output**: Residents, current assignments, and available staff members are displayed.
- **Preconditions**: Authorized Staff Member is logged into the system and has access to staff assignment data.
- **Postconditions**: System displays the staff assignment overview for the selected shift.

### Assign Staff to Resident
- **Input**: Resident, staff member, and shift.
- **Output**: Updated staff assignment overview and confirmation message.
- **Preconditions**: Authorized Staff Member has selected a Resident, selected one or more staff members, and selected a shift.
- **Postconditions**: StaffAssignment is created or updated, the Resident has at least one assigned staff member during the shift, and the change is logged in the audit trail.

### Update Staff Assignment
- **Input**: Resident, updated staff member assignment, and shift.
- **Output**: Updated staff assignment overview and confirmation message.
- **Preconditions**: Authorized Staff Member has opened an existing staff assignment for a shift.
- **Postconditions**: StaffAssignment is updated, the assignment overview is refreshed, and the change is logged in the audit trail.

### Validate Staff Assignment
- **Input**: Resident and assigned staff members.
- **Output**: Validation success or validation warning.
- **Preconditions**: Authorized Staff Member attempts to save a staff assignment.
- **Postconditions**: System confirms the assignment is valid or displays a warning if the Resident has no assigned staff member.
